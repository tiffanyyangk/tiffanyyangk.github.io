About Me

With a background in city planning, community development and design, I love working on designs that carry macro-level impact yet address our most personal needs. Here are a few notes about me:

I craft products and experiences.

I fell in love with experience design because its practice craves empathy, asks for a collaborative effort, and marries beauty with function.

I like digging deep and asking questions.

How can we leverage tech and design thinking to improve citywide systems? How can storytelling foster empathy between diverse people? I enjoy getting closer to the answers through my work.

I enjoy experimentation.

I play in prototypes and enjoy seeing new ideas come to life. I like piecing together solid research, an intuitive workflow and details that delight.

I like having the occasional day off, too.

I could spend days sketching in my favorite art museums, watching animated shorts and playing board games. Weekend picnics with volleyball, burritos and banh mi are definitely ok, too.
