BeerMapper

What are you drinking tonight?

BeerMapper is a tablet application that is looking to leverage its sophisticated algorithim that matches users to beer recommendations based on personal preferences. Our team sought to evaluate user benefits, streamline the app’s user flow and merge BeerMapper’s underlying technology with a simple user interface for the everyday beer enthusiast.
